Instruction Manual

Launch and manage your business page by text.

Each playbook includes: first-page setup, everyday upkeep, and support escalation.

How to use this guide Ready

Pick the business type closest to yours.

Use the setup and upkeep examples as a template, then adapt wording to your shop.

These are starter playbooks. ShopFront can support many service-based and local business categories.

Category 1

Restaurant / Cafe

Setup

First page launch

Text sequence:
1) "Hi"
2) "River Cafe"
3) "restaurant"
4) "Chicken Bowl $14, Veg Wrap $11, Latte $5, Cold Brew $6"
5) "Mon-Fri 7-8, Sat 8-9, Sun 8-4"
6) "245 Market St, San Francisco"
7) "Done"

Manage

Daily updates

Examples: "Add Chicken Bowl $14", "Change Latte to $5", "Open till 10 on Friday".

Support

When something breaks

Text: "Support: my menu is wrong". AI troubleshoots first and escalates to a human specialist when needed.
