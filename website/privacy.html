Privacy Policy

ShopFront ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices when using ShopFront.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the following information:

Phone number: used to identify and authenticate your ShopFront account.

Message content: SMS or WhatsApp message text you send to request updates to your website.

We do not collect additional personal information beyond the items listed above.

2. How We Use Information

We use your data only to operate the ShopFront service, specifically to:

- authenticate your ownership of the linked business website using your phone number, and

- process your message requests and apply updates to your linked website.

3. Data Retention

Message content is processed to complete your request and is not retained after processing.

4. Data Sharing and Marketing Use

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

We do not use your message content for third-party marketing, advertising, or promotional targeting.

5. Messaging Consent and Controls

By sending your first message to ShopFront, you consent to receive service-related responses needed to operate and manage your website.

You can control messaging at any time by texting the following commands:

- STOP: opt out of messages

- START: opt back in

- HELP: receive support information

6. Children’s Privacy

ShopFront is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. International Use

ShopFront is available globally. By using ShopFront, you understand that your information may be processed in countries where our service infrastructure operates.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
